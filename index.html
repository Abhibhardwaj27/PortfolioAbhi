About Me

I'm a final‑year Computer Science Engineering student who loves bringing ideas to life at the intersection of design, data, and code. My focus spans Frontend and Full‑Stack development with React, Node.js and Express, along with a growing body of work in Data Science and Machine Learning using Python. I enjoy shaping products that feel intuitive and look refined, balancing performance with a clean visual language.

Much of my learning comes from building real projects—iterating with user feedback, refining interactions, and automating workflows where it removes friction. I’m especially excited about how AI/ML can turn raw data into meaningful, human‑centred experiences, and I’m continuously exploring practical ways to embed intelligent features into modern web apps.

Beyond code, I’m curious by nature—often reading about Indian and European history or diving into economics. That broader perspective helps me think more critically and creatively when designing systems and solving problems in tech.
